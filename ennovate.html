Ennovate'24

Outline:

Ennovate empowers participants to explore an industry of their choice, uncover it's pivotal challenges, and devise an innovative business model to address it adeptly. Contestants must weave together a comprehensive BUSINESS PLAN that promise to reshape industries and elevate efficiency to unprecedented levels. With excitement building up , participants showcase their pitches, igniting the room with their strategic thinking and visionary SOLUTIONS.

This hybrid event comprises two rounds where participants must formulate comprehensive business plans, each serving as an integral component to their overall strategy. Contestants in this competition have the liberty to choose any industry, identify a challenge within its framework, and devise a solution by creating an optimal business model. The event concludes with the top three ideas earning well-deserved prize money, uniting diverse backgrounds to transform ideas into pragmatic solutions. It is the "Ultimate Business solution arena" hosted in KJ Somaiya College of Engineering!

  5. Round 2 will be conducted in offline mode in KJSCE. Shortlisted teams will present their ideas and business plans via a PowerPoint presentation to a panel of judges.

  1. The B-plan needs to be prepared according to the sample B-plan provided by the organizing team. Teams can write any additional points if required.

Rules For Round 2:

  2. Pitch submissions are required before the event.
  3. Participants should retain ownership of intellectual property.
  4. Participants should present their ideas using a ppt. Ideas must be original and copying of ideas would lead to disqualification.
  5. Each team will get "10 minutes" to present their pitch followed by a QNA session with the judges!

Judging Criteria:

    Problem Identification:

  1. Clarity of the chosen industry
  2. Understanding of the target audience
  3. Uniqueness of the identified problem

    4. Business Model Innovation:

  4. Feasibility of the proposed solution
  5. Value proposition & Competitive advantage

    6. Business Plan Structure & Completeness:

  6. Adherence to the provided format
  7. Conciseness of the plan
  8. Inclusion of elements like market analysis & strategies and financial estimates

    9. Scalability:

  9. Go-to-market Strategy and initial implementation plan

    10. Overall Presentation:

  10. Quality of writing & professionalism of 2-page pitch deck
