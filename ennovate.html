Ennovate

Outline:

Ennovate empowers participants to explore an industry of their choice, uncover it's pivotal challenges, and devise an innovative business model to address it adeptly. Contestants must weave together a comprehensive BUSINESS PLAN that promise to reshape industries and elevate efficiency to unprecedented levels. With excitement building up , participants showcase their pitches, igniting the room with their strategic thinking and visionary SOLUTIONS.

Guidelines:

  1. All registrations must happen via unstop.
  2. A person can register as a part of one team only. Multiple registrations will lead to disqualification.
  3. Ideas must be original and copying of ideas would lead to disqualification.
  4. The competition consists of two rounds. In the first round, participants must present a 2-page pitch deck (PDF Format) online via Unstop. The top 10 teams with the most promising ideas will advance to round 2.
  5. Round 2 will be conducted in offline mode in KJSCE. Shortlisted teams will present their ideas and business plans via a PowerPoint presentation to a panel of judges.

  7. In case of any discrepancy, the verdict of the organizing committee's would be considered as final.

Judging Criteria:

Participants will be ranked based on their ROI (Return on Investment) at the end of the competition. In case of a tie, the team with the lower number of trades will be given priority.
