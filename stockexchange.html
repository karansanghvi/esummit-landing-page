BloomBox Stock Exchange

Outline:

Bloombox Stock Exchange - BSE presented by Bloombox-E Cell, provides a virtual stock environment with a realistic replica of Dalal Street to help participants learn relevant skills needed for trading, with cash rewards for the top traders. Participants will also have a session on Mastering the Markets to learn more about this stock game during the course of the competition, guiding them through the terms and strategies. The event, EquityDash Duel, will take place for three days, during which participants can trade their stocks. One day prior to the event, a session will be conducted by PaperTrade where participants will be trained about stocks and the basic requirements needed for this event. The participant with the maximum net worth by the end of the competition wins.

Game Flow:

BSE is a virtual stock game where participants are allotted 5 lakh points at the start of the game, with which they can trade and perform all the trading activities practiced in the actual stock market. Players compete with each other to see who can predict the direction the stock markets will go next. Just like the actual stock market, this virtual stock market will be open for trading for a limited time each day, where participants can log in through an ID provided to them when they register on Unstop, the site on which this event will take place

Description:

Pick your side as Team Bear or Team Bull and dive into the BloomBox Stock Exchange, a thrilling three-day virtual challenge set on Dalal Street. Compete for cash rewards, gain expert guidance, and conquer the markets like never before!

Rules & Timeline:

  1. Participants must register for the event through Unstop.
  2. The account and assets allocated are purely virtual and cannot be converted into real money.
  3. Any profit or loss made in the contest is virtual and for competition purposes only.
  4. Duplicate registrations will not be accepted.
  5. Further details and guidelines will be communicated to participants via email.
  6. The event begins with an informative session conducted by PaperTrade, providing insights into the stock market and the game.
  7. Following the session, the event will span three days for trading activities.
  8. On the last day of the event, the participant with the highest net worth (cash in hand + value of stocks held) will be declared the winner, with the next two participants being the first and second runners-up, respectively.

Judging Criteria:

Participants will be ranked based on their ROI (Return on Investment) at the end of the competition. In case of a tie, the team with the lower number of trades will be given priority.
