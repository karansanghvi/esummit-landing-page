BloomBox Stock Exchange


Bloombox Stock Exchange - BSE presented by Bloombox-E Cell, provides a virtual stock environment with a realistic replica of Dalal Street to help participants learn relevant skills needed for trading, with cash rewards for the top traders. Participants will also have a session on Mastering the Markets to learn more about this stock game during the course of the competition, guiding them through the terms and strategies. The event, EquityDash Duel, will take place for three days, during which participants can trade their stocks. One day prior to the event, a session will be conducted by PaperTrade where participants will be trained about stocks and the basic requirements needed for this event. The participant with the maximum net worth by the end of the competition wins.
